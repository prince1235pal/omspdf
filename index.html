Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our PDF conversion service

Is this service free for commercial use?

Our omPDF Converter Pro is available with both free and premium plans. The free version includes basic conversion features, while our enterprise plans offer advanced options, higher volume processing, and priority support.

How is file security handled?

We take security seriously. All files are processed in isolated environments, automatically deleted after conversion (typically within an hour), and we use enterprise-grade encryption throughout the process. We do not inspect file contents.

What are the file size limitations?

Standard accounts can convert Word documents up to 20MB each and image files up to 10MB each, with batch processing of up to 10 Word files or 20 images simultaneously. Enterprise accounts have higher limits.

How long does the conversion process take?

Most conversions complete within seconds. Larger files or complex documents may take longer to process. Our enterprise plans include priority processing for faster results.

Does this service work on mobile devices?

Yes, our omPDF Converter Pro is cloud-based and fully responsive, working on all modern devices including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones across all major operating systems and browsers.
